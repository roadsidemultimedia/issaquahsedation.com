Combined restorative Cosmetic Procedures:
Full Coverage Porcealin And Porcelain Fused-To-Metal Crowns

When the structural integrity of a tooth has been compromised by "old filling materials "cracks and decay, a full coverage "crown"or "cap" is the indicated treatment. If the restoration is subject to display by the patient when talking or smiling ,the" tooth-colored" porcelain crown or porcelain fused-to-metal crown is t he treatment of choice.

 

Number Of Office Visits TWO
Anesthetic Requirements YES
Treatment
Estimated Longevity		 DECADES
Cosmetic Value HIGH
Insurance Benefits YES (Consult Insurance Co.)

The advantages are: restored function, durability and cosmetics. The disadvantages are: tooth reduction and cost.

Combined restorative Cosmetic Procedures:
Cast-Gold Inlays And Onlays

Restorative situations involving broken and damaged teeth where function and durability requirements exceed cosmetic requirements, the cast-gold inlay and onlay restorations remain the treatment of choice.

 

Number Of Office Visits TWO
Anesthetic Requirements YES
Treatment
Estimated Longevity		 DECADES
Cosmetic Value NON-NEGATIVE
Insurance Benefits YES (Consult Insurance Co.)

The advantages of this technique are: minimal tooth reduction , restored function and greater durability . The disadvantages are: poor dental cosmetics and cost.
