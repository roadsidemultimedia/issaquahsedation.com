Dental Misfortune and Neglect:
HEREDITY, DIET AND TOTAL HYGIENE NEGLECT

Heredity diet and oral hygiene play a major role in the health and appearance of your dental condition.

Occasionally patients from third world countries display the ravaging dental disfigurements associated with their own heredity, in combination with poor dietary standards and total dental hygiene neglect.

These photographs lend credence primarily to the significance of proper diet and dental hygiene habits.

Although these dental conditions are usually treatable with today's restorative and cosmetic techniques, the procedures are complicated and expensive.

DENTAL MISFORTUNE AND NEGLECT: THE DENTAL PROSTHESIS FALSE - ALTERNATIVE

This photograph of dental prosthetic appliances belongs to a generation of" dental cripples" who fell victim to significant dental problems, most of which were due to inadequate materials, poor techniques and lack of knowledge.

These prosthetic appliances failures graphically illustrate the inability of this dental alternative to meet and expectations of the patient.

Fortunately, due to improved materials, advanced techniques, and greater knowledge, modern dentistry has essentially overcome this potential dental disability for all responsible patients.
