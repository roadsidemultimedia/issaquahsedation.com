TAKE HOME WHITENING

This procedure is achieved by utilizing a custom fabricated bleaching tray and a Dentist Dispensed, safe whitening agent.

 

Number Of Office Visits TWO
Anesthetic Requirements NONE
Treatment
Estimated Longevity		 5 TO 10 YEARS
Cosmetic Value HIGH
Insurance Benefits NONE

This treatment is accomplished by the patient wearing the bleaching tray with the whitening agent in the tray for six to eight nights while sleeping (six to eight hours each night).

Results on yellow and/or brown teeth are most dramatic!
