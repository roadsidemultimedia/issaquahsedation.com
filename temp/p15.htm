LAB-FABRICATED ADHESION DENTISTRY: ADHESION FIXED BRIDGES

 

The concept of adhesion dentistry has quite naturally expanded to fulfill the demand of fixed bridgework but on a limited basis.

 

If the abutment teeth are free of both decay and previously placed dental restorations, the patients may be a candidate for noninvasive adhesion short-span porcelain/composite or porcelain-fused-to-metal adhesion bridges.

 

The following photographs are example of adhesion bridgework in porcelain/composite and porcelain-fused-metal.

 

Patient’s concern: function, realistic cosmetic results, and invasion of adjacent healthy teeth.
