ABOUT US

 

Dr. Rehan Bashar  and Dr. Ajay Dhankhar  are classmates from School of Dentistry, Loma Linda University, California. After serving in various counties of Washington in the past three years, they teamed to make Issaquah Valley Dental Care a paradigm, catering to all your needs. They bring with them a combined experience of 25 years. They share a common philosophy and vision of providing comprehensive dental care under one roof with minimal visits.

 

They have devoted countless hours towards  continuing education courses in latest dental advances. They are committed to providing outstanding dental care and an exceptional dental experience for everyone.

 

All members of Issaquah Valley Dental Care team share the same commitment to excellence and stand by the philosophy of putting patients’ wishes and desires first because every individual is unique.
